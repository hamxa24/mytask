Why Choose Softlogi for Salesforce Consulting & Advisory Services?

At Softlogi, we are committed to delivering innovative Salesforce solutions that accelerate business transformation. With a proven track record of empowering organizations, we enable you to unlock the full potential of Salesforce across your enterprise.

  • Comprehensive Product Selection Guidance: With the ever-expanding range of tools like AppExchange packages and integration technologies such as Mulesoft, Boomi, Celigo, Oracle, or Informatica, we help you navigate and select the optimal solution for your business needs.
  • Seamless CPQ Transformation: We enable seamless Configure Price Quote (CPQ) solutions, driving efficiency across Sales, Finance, and Service teams. Our CPQ transformations accelerate sales velocity and ensure data continuity, delivering a unified customer experience.

  • Organizational Change Management: Recognizing that people are key to successful technology adoption, our Change Management service assesses organizational readiness, formulates strategies to enhance user experience, and facilitates smoother transitions at all levels.
  • Advanced Analytics and Einstein: With Tableau integrated into Salesforce, we provide enhanced analytics capabilities, enabling faster decision-making and improving deal closure and case resolution times.
